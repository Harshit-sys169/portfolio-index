## Advanced Customization Ideas

### 1. Discussion Categories (optional)
For each repo with discussions enabled, create these categories:
- **Questions**: Ask for help or clarifications
- **Ideas**: Discuss improvements or extensions
- **Learnings**: Share findings and insights
- **Collaborations**: Propose partnerships or contributions

### 2. Welcome Discussion Post
Create a pinned discussion in top projects like:
```

Body:
This is a space to discuss the project, ask questions, and share insights.

Feel free to:
- Ask about the methodology
- Discuss potential improvements
- Share your own applications
- Suggest datasets or extensions
```
